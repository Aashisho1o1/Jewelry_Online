# Product Photo Capture Checklist

Use this file during the shoot. The goal is simple: get clean, consistent images that fit the storefront without guessing.

Reference detail list: [PHOTO_SHOTLIST.md](./PHOTO_SHOTLIST.md)

Quick-print version: [PHOTO_CAPTURE_PRINTABLE.md](./PHOTO_CAPTURE_PRINTABLE.md)

After the shoot: [PHOTO_UPLOAD_CHECKLIST.md](./PHOTO_UPLOAD_CHECKLIST.md)

## Before the shoot

- Clean every piece with a soft cloth.
- Remove fingerprints, dust, loose threads, and sticker marks.
- Prepare one plain light background.
- Prepare one gifting surface or jewelry box for gift shots.
- Keep a white card or foam board nearby to bounce light.
- Keep ring holders, necklace tape, and earring stands ready if needed.
- Rename products before shooting so file naming stays consistent.

## If using a phone

- Clean the lens before every product.
- Use the rear camera, not the selfie camera.
- Prefer `1x` or `2x`. Do not use ultra-wide.
- Turn grid on.
- Tap to focus on the jewelry, then lower exposure slightly if silver looks blown out.
- Hold the phone parallel to the product for hero shots.
- Use portrait orientation for storefront images.
- Take 2 to 4 versions of each shot so you can choose the sharpest one later.

## If using a camera

- Use soft diffused light.
- Keep white balance consistent across the whole session.
- Use a tripod when possible.
- Stop down enough to keep the full product sharp in hero shots.
- Take one safety frame slightly brighter and one slightly darker for reflective silver pieces.

## Required framing

- Main storefront shot: `4:5`
- Worn shot: `4:5`
- Detail shot: square or tight `4:5`
- Angle shot: `4:5`
- Gift shot: `4:5`

## Shot order for each product

1. Main hero shot
2. Detail shot
3. Worn shot
4. Angle shot
5. Gift / packaging shot if relevant

## Quick quality check after each product

- Product is centered and straight.
- Silver is not overexposed.
- Stone or clasp details are sharp.
- Background is clean and not distracting.
- No skin dryness, dust, or lint visible in worn shots.
- At least one image clearly shows scale.
- File names match the product id.

## File naming rule

- Main: `{product-id}-main.jpg`
- Worn: `{product-id}-worn.jpg`
- Detail: `{product-id}-detail.jpg`
- Angle: `{product-id}-angle.jpg`
- Gift: `{product-id}-gift.jpg`
