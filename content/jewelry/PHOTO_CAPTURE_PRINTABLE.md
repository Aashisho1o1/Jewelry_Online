## Setup

- Clean the product
- Clean the lens
- Use a plain light background
- Use soft light
- Keep the product straight
- Shoot vertical for storefront images

## Phone Rules

- Use rear camera
- Use `1x` or `2x`
- Turn grid on
- Tap to focus on the jewelry
- Lower exposure slightly if silver looks too bright
- Take 2 to 4 frames of each shot

## 5 Required Shots

1. Main hero shot
2. Detail close-up
3. Worn shot
4. Angle shot
5. Gift or box shot

## Quality Check

- Sharp focus
- No dust or fingerprints
- No harsh glare
- Product centered
- Scale is clear
- File name matches product id
