## Global standards

- Use a clean neutral background for the main product shot.
- Capture at least one worn shot for rings, bracelets, necklaces, and earrings.
- Keep the main storefront image near a `4:5` ratio.
- Capture one square close-up for PDP galleries and collection cards.
- Use soft, even lighting. Avoid harsh reflections and deep shadows.
- Export web-ready images under `500 KB` when possible.

## File naming

- Main image: `{product-id}-main.jpg`
- Worn image: `{product-id}-worn.jpg`
- Close-up: `{product-id}-detail.jpg`
- Angle view: `{product-id}-angle.jpg`
- Packaging / gifting: `{product-id}-gift.jpg`

## Required shot types for every product

1. Front hero shot on neutral background.
2. Close-up detail shot of texture, clasp, stone, or setting.
3. Worn shot to show scale.
4. Slight angle shot for depth.
5. Gift / box / packaging shot if gifting is a selling point.
